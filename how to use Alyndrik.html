Have Issues with Installing Alyndrik?

Alyndrik Lite is designed for Android versions 10 and above.

How to Fix Installation Problems:

  1. Storage Space: Although the Alyndrik.apk file is 2.0 MB, ensure your device has sufficient free storage capacity before attempting installation.
  2. Android Version: Verify that your Android device is running version 10 or higher, as Alyndrik is only compatible with these versions.
05

Alyndrik Lite Not Working?

  1. Initial Internet Connection: While Alyndrik functions both online and offline, it initially requires an active internet connection for at least 10 minutes to set up.
  2. Cached Data: Do not clear Alyndrik's cached data. If cleared, you will need to reconnect to the internet to download necessary files again.
  3. Usage Limits: Alyndrik Lite operates with usage limits. Excessive use within a short timeframe may temporarily take it offline until the system's quota resets after a 12-hour period. Therefore, please use it efficiently.
  4. Software Update: Make sure your Android device is updated to the latest software version.
  5. Connectivity: If your data connection drops to 0 MBs, toggle Flight mode (ON & OFF), then Restart the app.
  6. SIM Cards to Use: Verify that your Airtel 4G SIM card is selected for mobile data.
  7. Stable Network: Ensure you have a Stable Network Connection.
